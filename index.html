What is ClearText AI?

ClearText AI is a tool primarily designed to support users with cognitive disabilities by summarizing complex web content into shorter, simpler sentences. However, all users can benefit from its features, including highlighting key points, providing audio narration to aid memory, and offering interactive definitions to support learning and comprehension.

  • 🔍 Text simplification: AI rewrites content into clear, concise language that's easier to understand
  • 🔊 Text-to-speech integration: Users can listen to content
  • 🧠 Key point summarizer: Pulls out important ideas from a wall of text
  • 📚 Word explainer: Identify and define complex words (In development)
